# Report Evidence

## Timetable Visualisation Export

The scheduler automatically exports calendar-style programme, tutor and room timetable views from the validated scheduled assignments.

This is an output feature only. It does not reinterpret scheduling requirements, rerun the scheduler, alter fixed-session handling, change demand reconciliation, or modify Template 2 field mappings.

## Interpretation

The visual workbooks support stakeholder review by converting the validated timetable into readable weekly views. They make programme-year completeness, tutor workload and room occupancy easier to inspect while keeping unresolved demand in exception reports.
